HOW TO USE THESE COVER LETTERS

Every letter has [bracketed placeholders] — fill these in before sending:

• [Company Name] — the actual company you are applying to
• [Position] — the exact job title from the posting
• [specific reason] — one real, researched reason you want THIS company
• [specific area] — their actual product, research, or service you reference
• [date] — your available start date

What NOT to do:

• Do not send without filling every placeholder — a letter addressed to '[Company Name]' is worse than no letter
• Do not use the same letter for two companies — the whole point is specificity
• Do not translate English letters through Google Translate — use the pre-written Japanese/Korean/Chinese versions instead
• The Japanese, Korean, and Chinese native-language letters MUST be reviewed by a native speaker before sending

The one thing that makes these letters work:

Research the company for 15 minutes before filling in [specific reason]. Look at their recent work, a paper they published, a product they launched, or a problem they are solving. One genuine, specific sentence about why you want to work there does more than four paragraphs of generic enthusiasm.
